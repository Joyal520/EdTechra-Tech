What is a Computer?

A computer is an electronic machine that receives information, processes it, and produces results.

Computers help us:

How it Works: The IPO Cycle

Input ‚Üí Process ‚Üí Output. It's the secret to every machine!

1

INPUT

Information entered into the computer.

2

PROCESSING

The computer thinks and calculates (The Brain/CPU).

3

OUTPUT

The final result shown to you.

Input Devices

Used to give data or commands to the computer.

Keyboard

Used for typing letters, numbers, and giving commands.

Mouse

Helps you select, move, and click on objects on the screen.

Microphone

Records your voice or sounds and sends them to the computer.

Output Devices

Used to show the results of processing.

Monitor

Displays images, videos, and text clearly for you to see.

Printer

Prints your digital work onto actual paper.

Speakers

Produces music, voice, and other sounds for you to hear.

Hardware vs Software

Hardware

The physical parts you can touch.

  • Monitor & Keyboard
  • Mouse & CPU
  • Internal parts like RAM

Software

Digital instructions you cannot touch.

  • Windows / macOS
  • Web browsers (Chrome)
  • Apps & Video Games
