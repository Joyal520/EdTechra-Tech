What is a Prompt?

A Prompt is the instruction or question you give to an AI. Prompt Engineering is the art of making those instructions so clear that the AI gives you exactly what you need.

Role "Act as a teacher"
Context "In a 5th grade class"
Task "Explain gravity"
Format "Using a short poem"

// Bad Prompt

"Write a story."

// Expert Prompt

"Act as a sci-fi writer. Write a 200-word story about a robot who discovers a flower on Mars. Keep the tone hopeful and use simple words."
