Phishing Red Flags

Panic & Urgency

Messages like "Do it in 1 hour!" or "Your account is suspended!" are designed to make you act without thinking.

Strange Sender

Check the email address carefully. A real company uses their name, not random letters or "gmail.com".

Asking for Secrets

No real company will ever ask for your Password or OTP through an email or link.
